About Me

A Visionary Leader
Shaping Nigeria's Future

Paschal Duru is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Podium Industries Limited, one of Nigeria's foremost companies specialising in the design, supply, installation, and maintenance of low voltage electronic security and safety systems. With over a decade and a half of dedicated service, he has built an organisation that stands at the intersection of innovation, integrity, and excellence.

Under his visionary leadership, Podium Industries has grown from a local enterprise into a trusted partner for individuals, private and public establishments, banks, financial institutions, government agencies, and international organisations — including the oil and gas sector. Paschal's philosophy is simple: "Preserve lives, protect property, and exceed every expectation."

His deep technical expertise, coupled with a relentless drive for quality, has earned Podium Industries an outstanding reputation for reliability and service excellence across Nigeria.
